# Public Safety Rules

This repo can discuss investment research systems, trading frameworks, and crypto infrastructure. It cannot publish private financial reality or tell anyone what to buy, sell, hold, transfer, stake, bridge, or sign.

## Do Publish

- Public repo links and metadata.
- Research categories and tool maps.
- Simulation and paper-trading patterns.
- Risk labels and human approval gates.
- Mock memos and templates.

## Do Not Publish

- Private positions, balances, accounts, or broker data.
- Wallet addresses tied to real holdings.
- Seed phrases, private keys, API secrets, or recovery material.
- Live order instructions or target allocations.
- Performance promises or expected-return claims.
- Tax, legal, securities, accounting, or compliance conclusions.

## Required Boundary

Use this language near investor and trading workflows:

> This is research organization and system architecture, not financial, investment, legal, tax, accounting, or securities advice. Humans retain authority over capital and execution.

## Risk Labels

- `research-only`: read-only research and source analysis.
- `simulation`: backtesting or paper workflows.
- `execution-adjacent`: can connect to brokers, exchanges, or order systems if misused.
- `custody-adjacent`: touches wallets, signing, multisig, or recovery concepts.
- `regulated-adjacent`: may be misunderstood as advice without clear human review.
